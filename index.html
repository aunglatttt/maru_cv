National Immigration & Border Control System

Role at: Secure Link Company

Developed and maintained a comprehensive system for managing passenger data at international airports, enhancing national security and immigration processes.

My Role & Contributions:

  • Developed the primary counter application using Windows Forms (C#) for capturing passenger data.
  • Engineered a robust data synchronization mechanism: data flowed from airport counters to local Linux-based airport servers, and then aggregated to a central server.
  • Implemented an offline data handling capability to ensure data integrity during internet disruptions, queuing passenger data for subsequent transmission.
  • Utilized SignalR to deliver real-time blacklist information from the central server to airport counters.
  • Contributed to the central server's functionalities, including management of visa types, stay durations, and monitoring tools for passenger statistics.
  • Integrated the "Form C" system (for tracking foreigner accommodation) with the central server.
